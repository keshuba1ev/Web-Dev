Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 15–20 min
  • Serves: 2–3
  • Best for: breakfast
Ingredients Quantity
Flour1 cup
Baking powder2 tsp
Sugar1–2 tbsp
Saltpinch
Milk3/4 cup
Egg1
Melted butter (or oil)2 tbsp
  1. Step 1: Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Step 2: Whisk milk + egg + melted butter.
  3. Step 3: Combine wet + dry. Don’t overmix.
  4. Step 4: Cook on a lightly oiled pan 1–2 min per side.

Source: Wikipedia (Pancake)

Quick Guacamole

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 2–4
  • Best for: snack / tacos
Ingredients Quantity
Ripe avocados2
Lime juice1–2 tbsp
Saltto taste
Tomato (optional)1/2, diced
Onion (optional)2 tbsp, chopped
Chili/pepper (optional)to taste
  1. Step 1: Mash avocados with a fork.
  2. Step 2: Add lime juice + salt.
  3. Step 3: Mix in tomato/onion/chili if you want.
  4. Step 4: Taste, adjust salt, serve immediately.

Source: Wikipedia (Guacamole)

Pasta Aglio e Olio

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 2
  • Best for: quick dinner
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti200 g
Olive oil3–4 tbsp
Garlic3–5 cloves, sliced
Chili flakes (optional)to taste
Saltfor pasta water
Parsley (optional)small handful
  1. Step 1: Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Step 2: Gently heat olive oil, add garlic (don’t burn).
  3. Step 3: Add chili flakes if you like.
  4. Step 4: Toss pasta with the oil/garlic. Add a splash of pasta water if needed.

Source: Wikipedia (Aglio e olio)
